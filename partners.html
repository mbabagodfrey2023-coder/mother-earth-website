// THE MISSION TEST

What We Look For
in a Partner

Every partnership passes the same three-question test before we formalise anything. These are non-negotiable — they come from the Purpose Trust, not from us.

01
Mission Alignment

Does this partnership strengthen our ability to defend the planet? Partnerships that dilute the environmental mission — for any reason — are rejected regardless of commercial value.

02
Data Integrity

Any data partner must provide verifiable, source-cited data. We do not use manipulated, commercially biassed, or unverifiable datasets. Our agents flag and reject non-credible sources.

03
Independence Preserved

No partner — regardless of size or investment — can direct our agents' outputs, alter our methodology, or influence our published findings. The Golden Share enforces this constitutionally.

04
Transparency by Default

Partnerships are disclosed publicly on this page within 30 days of signing. We don't do shadow partnerships. If you need confidentiality, we likely aren't the right fit.

05
Long-term Thinking

We are building a 50-year organisation. We prefer partners who think in decades, not quarters. Short-term transactional relationships don't serve a planetary mission.

06
Kenya Jurisdiction

All formal partnership agreements are governed by Kenyan law and VASP Act 2025 compliance. Partners working in regulated sectors must meet our KYC and AML standards under ATLAS oversight.
