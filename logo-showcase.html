03 · Mark Anatomy

Globe Circle

The filled circle gives the mark mass and instant planetary recognition. At any size from 16px to billboard, you read "Earth" immediately — no guessing required.

Prime Meridian + Equatorial Arc

One vertical line (0° longitude, the prime meridian through Africa) crosses one horizontal arc (the equator). The dashed back-arc adds depth — you're seeing a sphere, not a flat circle.

Latitude Graticules

Tropic of Cancer + 20°S latitude lines, rendered as subtle dashed arcs. They encode precision without adding noise — this is a cartographic instrument, not a logo clip-art.

Kenya Node

Orange focal dot at 37°E on the equatorial arc — Kenya's exact longitude. It sits on the front face of the globe (lower half of equatorial ellipse). The glow creates a "live signal" quality.
