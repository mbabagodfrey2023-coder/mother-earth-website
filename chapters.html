// HOW IT WORKS

Local sovereignty.
Shared mission.

Each chapter is an independently incorporated entity with its own Purpose Trust, local board, and country-specific compliance — all bound to the same planetary mandate. No franchise fee. No revenue extraction. The mission is the only shareholder.

01 / STRUCTURE
Local Purpose Trust
Every chapter incorporates in its home jurisdiction with a Purpose Trust holding a Golden Share veto over environmental decisions. The mission cannot be diluted, sold, or redirected by any investor or government.
02 / TECHNOLOGY
Shared 33-agent framework
Mother Earth Kenya provides the AI agent architecture, the IRIS data stack, and the NEXUS blockchain layer to every chapter at zero cost. You operate; we maintain the planetary infrastructure.
03 / DATA
Country-specific intelligence
Each chapter monitors its own ecosystem — sensors, satellite feeds, regulatory landscape, climate risk. Data is sovereign to the chapter. Aggregate insights feed the global mesh, but local data stays local.
04 / GOVERNANCE
SAGE oversight network
A federated council of chapter SAGE agents reviews cross-border decisions affecting more than one nation. No single chapter can override another's sovereignty. Disputes go to the global Purpose Trust panel.
05 / FUNDING
Local revenue, local mission
Each chapter operates its own IRIS data marketplace, sets local pricing, and retains 100% of subscription revenue. Profits fund local environmental work and chapter operations. No remittance to the founding chapter.
06 / RECOGNITION
"Mother Earth [Country]" brand
Approved chapters use the Mother Earth name, seal, and brand framework under a perpetual royalty-free licence — conditional only on maintaining the Purpose Trust structure and Golden Share integrity.

// APPLY TO LEAD

Lead Mother Earth in your country.

We're looking for founders, environmental scientists, lawyers, and civic-tech operators ready to anchor a Mother Earth chapter. Approval is rigorous — every chapter represents the entire network — but the framework, tech, and brand are ready when you are.

  • Local incorporation — you handle company registration in your jurisdiction
  • Purpose Trust adoption — Golden Share filed in your country's trust law
  • Minimum viable team — at minimum 1 environmental scientist + 1 legal counsel before chapter activation
  • 90-day onboarding — joint Mother Earth Kenya team works alongside you to deploy your 33 agents
  • Year-1 transparency — public mission log + audited financials for first 12 months
  • No franchise fee, ever — we don't extract revenue from chapters
