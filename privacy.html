7. Your Rights

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019, you have the right to:

Access

Request a copy of your personal data (we respond within 21 days)

Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate data

Erasure

Request deletion where we no longer have a lawful basis

Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for marketing

Portability

Receive your data in a machine-readable format

Restrict Processing

Request restriction while a complaint is investigated

Withdraw Consent

At any time, without affecting prior processing

To exercise your rights, email privacy@motherearth.systems with subject "Data Rights Request".

If unsatisfied, you may complain to the ODPC at www.odpc.go.ke.
