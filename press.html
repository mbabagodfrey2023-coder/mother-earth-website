LONG · 165 WORDS

Mother Earth Kenya is the world's first AI-native environmental governance organisation. Headquartered in Nairobi, it operates 33 autonomous AI agents across 7 specialist branches: AMARA (legal & compliance), NEXUS (blockchain & technology), GAIA (environmental intelligence), ATLAS (treasury & finance), IRIS (data marketplace), SAGE (governance & trust), and ECHO (communications & growth).

The organisation is governed by a Mother Earth Environmental Purpose Trust whose Golden Share holds absolute veto over any decision that would compromise the planetary mission. The Trust structure means Mother Earth cannot be acquired, redirected, or hollowed out — its mission is constitutionally locked.

Mother Earth operates a global chapter model. Kenya is the founding chapter. Every country is invited to launch its own, governed by its own local Purpose Trust, sharing the same 33-agent technology framework with zero franchise fee. The organisation publishes every agent decision in real time at motherearth.systems/mission-control.
