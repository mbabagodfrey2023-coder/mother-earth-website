// TREASURY POLICY · ATLAS DOMAIN

How the books will be kept.

Adopted from the Financial Controls Policy v1.0 + Investment Policy Statement (IPS). Activates automatically the day Mother Earth Kenya Limited receives its certificate of incorporation.

// AUTHORITY GATES
Single-signature ceiling
Treasury operations up to KES 50,000 may be executed autonomously by ATLAS SWEEP. Above that threshold, multi-signature workflow activates (CEO + ATLAS WALL co-signature). Any transaction ≥ KES 130 million triggers Golden Share Critical Gate review — human approval mandatory regardless of context.
ENFORCED BY: ATLAS · CEO CRITICAL_GATES
// CUSTODY
Hot / cold split + multi-sig
Operating wallet (hot) holds ≤ 90 days runway for day-to-day liabilities. Reserve wallet (cold) holds remainder under 2-of-3 hardware-backed multi-sig. KEYS module (NEXUS) air-gapped signing. SWEEP rebalances daily from hot to cold if hot exceeds threshold.
ENFORCED BY: NEXUS KEYS · ATLAS SWEEP
// EXPOSURE LIMITS
No single asset > 60%
Cash / stablecoin baseline. No single counterparty exposure exceeds 30% of treasury. No single asset class exceeds 60%. WALL agent monitors IPS breach in real time and auto-freezes outflows on violation pending CEO review.
ENFORCED BY: ATLAS WALL
// FX
KES / USD / stablecoin
Operating cash held primarily in KES (Kenyan Shillings) post-BRS via M-Pesa / commercial bank. International customer revenue settled in USD. Reserve held in regulated stablecoin once VASP licence active. No exotic currency exposure; no derivatives.
ENFORCED BY: ATLAS WALL · TAX
// COMPLIANCE
Kenya Revenue Authority alignment
TAX agent maintains parallel ledger ready for KRA filing. VAT registration on certificate of incorporation. Withholding tax on cross-border supplier payments. Quarterly tax provisioning ring-fenced from operating cash. Annual external audit from Y1 close.
ENFORCED BY: ATLAS TAX · AMARA CLOCK
// REPORTING CADENCE
Monthly + quarterly + annual
Internal monthly close (ATLAS YIELD). Quarterly Trustee briefing once Trust registered. Annual audited financial statements published publicly on this page from Y1 close (December 2026). All Sentinel + Planetary customer flows reconciled per cycle.
ENFORCED BY: ATLAS YIELD · SAGE BOARD
// FUNDING SOURCES · WHERE THE MONEY COMES FROM

No equity. No debt. Yet.

Mother Earth Kenya does not raise equity capital. The Purpose Trust structure means there are no shareholders to return capital to. Funding comes from the four sources below, in order of priority.

🌱

Founder Loan (pre-incorporation)

Andy advances personal funds at 0% interest to cover pre-incorporation operating expenses. Codified in the Consultancy Agreement (Clause 4B). Repayable at the Consultant's discretion subject to a KES 200,000 minimum cash reserve. Recorded as a liability on Day 1 of the company books.

ACTIVE · ~KES 47K
💎

IRIS Marketplace Revenue

Subscription tiers — Guardian ($99/mo), Sentinel ($499/mo), Planetary ($1,499/mo) — plus Research tier (free for accredited institutions). Primary long-term revenue source. Activated post-BRS once Stripe + M-Pesa rails go live (Q3 2026).

UNBLOCKS POST-BRS
🤝

Mission-aligned grant capital

Targeted grant applications for specific environmental monitoring infrastructure (e.g. additional GAIA sensor stations, regional chapter launches). No general-operating grants — grant capital is restricted-purpose only, with public reporting on disbursement.

PLANNED · 2027
🌍

Chapter network contribution

Voluntary contributions from international chapters to shared planetary infrastructure (federated SAGE, NEXUS chain anchoring, shared agent maintenance). Not a franchise fee — each chapter sets its own contribution level, with transparency on use of pooled funds.

PLANNED · 2027+
