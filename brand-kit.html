06 — Brand Voice

Mother Earth Kenya speaks with the confidence of a regulated institution and the clarity of an engineer. Never preach. Never hype. State facts that command respect.

✓ DO
  • Rooted in Kenya. Monitoring the planet.
  • 33 agents. One mission.
  • Environmental intelligence, governed by trust.
  • Precision over poetry.
  • State the fact, then the implication.
✗ DON'T
  • Saving the planet one algorithm at a time! 🌱
  • We're passionate about sustainability.
  • Leveraging cutting-edge AI synergies.
  • Game-changing disruptive innovation.
  • Exclamation marks in professional copy.

08 — Usage Rules

Clear Space

Maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the "M" in Mother Earth Kenya on all sides of the logo.

Minimum Size

Icon only: 16×16px minimum. Full lockup: 180px wide minimum. Never use below these sizes.

Never Do This

Do not stretch, rotate, recolour, add drop shadows, place on busy photography, or use unauthorised font substitutes.
