Phase 2 — Fable 5 Capabilities

These 11 tasks require reasoning depth, multi-modal data synthesis, and autonomous execution beyond current AI. Data pipelines are being built now. Intelligence layer deploys with Fable 5.

F5-001 · HIGHEST PRIORITY 🛰️

Dark Carbon Ledger

Cross-correlate satellite thermal signatures, AIS global shipping transponder data, energy grid consumption, and SEC/EU ETS financial disclosures to surface unreported emissions at the entity level. Output: verified intelligence briefs, not accusations. AMARA gates every publication.

Requires simultaneous multi-modal reasoning across 4 incompatible data formats. Current models cannot maintain coherence at this cross-domain synthesis depth.
F5-002 · GAIA FLAGSHIP 🌐

Predictive Cascade Modelling

Extend the current tipping point engine from "what is the stress now" to "if Amazon deforestation reaches 22%, what is the probability of AMOC weakening within 5 years?" Real-time probabilistic cascade forecasting with confidence intervals from IPCC scenarios.

Requires sustained probabilistic reasoning over a directed graph with 9 nodes, non-linear feedback loops, and uncertainty propagation. Beyond current chain-of-thought depth.
F5-003 · CREDIBILITY ENGINE 📄

Autonomous Research Agent

Fable 5 formulates original environmental hypotheses from GAIA data, runs statistical analysis, writes peer-reviewed papers, and submits to open-access journals. Human scientist signs as corresponding author. Mother Earth Kenya becomes a publishing institution.

Requires closed-loop scientific reasoning: hypothesis → experiment design → analysis → paper. No current model has demonstrated reliable novel discovery in this loop.
F5-004 · MOST RADICAL 🐋

Animal Behavioral Early Warning

Decode animal stress signals as planetary early warning — whale song frequency shifts (NOAA PMEL hydrophones), bird migration deviation (Cornell Lab eBird API), coral spawning timing changes (AIMS reef sensors). Animals precede satellite data by weeks. No instrument detects what a billion years of evolution already knows.

Requires cross-species signal interpretation across acoustic, movement, and biological data. Genuine frontier — no existing system attempts this at scale.
F5-005 💬

Natural Language Data Query

"Show me Arctic sea ice trend for the last 10 years vs Amazon fire frequency, with AMOC transport as a third axis." Fable 5 interprets plain-language requests, joins multi-source datasets, and returns charts + narrative in one pass.

Requires multi-source data joining with semantic understanding of environmental variable relationships. Current models produce plausible but unreliable cross-domain queries.
F5-006 ⚖️

Environmental Litigation Intelligence

AMARA + Fable 5 build the world's most comprehensive environmental law database: precedents, plaintiffs, outcomes, and novel legal arguments across 190 jurisdictions. Output: litigation strategy briefs for NGOs and governments. Published as intelligence, not legal advice.

Requires synthesis of case law across languages, jurisdictions, and decades. Current models hallucinate citations under sustained legal reasoning pressure.
F5-007 🌍

Multi-Language Chapter Portals

Each of the 9 chapter countries (USA, Brazil, South Africa, Nigeria, UK, India, France, Indonesia, Canada) gets a localised portal in their primary language — not just translated, but culturally adapted. Content, regulatory framing, and data emphasis adapted per jurisdiction.

Requires sustained cultural and regulatory context-switching across 9 markets simultaneously. Beyond current translation-only approaches.
F5-008 📊

Real-Time Ecosystem Valuation

Price ecosystem services in real time as tradeable IRIS assets: Amazon pollination ($X/acre/day), Atlantic fisheries stock ($/tonne carbon-equivalent), reef bleaching impact ($/km² loss). ATLAS prices these. NEXUS tokenises them. IRIS trades them.

Requires real-time integration of ecological, economic, and financial models with live sensor data. No standard methodology exists — Fable 5 derives it from first principles.
F5-009 🔍

Chapter Applicant AI Screening

When a chapter application comes in (9 countries active, more incoming), Fable 5 screens: founder credibility, local regulatory environment, technical capacity, strategic fit, conflict of interest. Returns a structured recommendation to SAGE within 60 seconds.

Requires integrating public data, application content, and jurisdictional context into a governance-grade decision. Current models produce inconsistent risk scores.
F5-010 🏛️

Institutional Pitch Generator

A climate fund, university, or government arrives at motherearth.systems. Fable 5 identifies them (via domain/IP), generates a custom pitch deck, data sample, and contract term sheet in their language and regulatory context — automatically, before a human has been in touch.

Requires prospect identification, dynamic document generation, and jurisdictional customisation in a single coherent pass. Current models fail on the integration step.
F5-011 · ECHO FLAGSHIP · NEW 🎬

YouTube Avatar Content Generator

GAIA detects a natural calamity event — wildfire breakout, Arctic ice collapse, flood surge, coral bleaching alert. Fable 5 drafts a science-backed 3–5 minute script in Mother Earth's brand voice. Andy Godfrey's digital avatar (HeyGen API) renders the video. CEO reviews before upload. YouTube Data API v3 auto-publishes to the Mother Earth Kenya channel. ECHO posts a 60-second clip to X + LinkedIn the same day.

Content pillars: natural calamity breakdowns · monthly tipping point status · Earth status reports · "Built in Africa" storytelling

Requires real-time multi-modal reasoning: GAIA sensor data → narrative script → avatar video generation with consistent scientific accuracy and brand voice. CEO approval CC (andygodfrey@motherearth.systems) required on every video before upload. First 10 videos manually approved; ECHO autonomy unlocked after trust is established.
